Barber Master Suite Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Barber Master Suite is a barbershop management app designed to help barbers manage appointments, clients, services, notes, and local backups directly on their device.

Data Collection

Barber Master Suite does not collect, transmit, or share personal data with the developer or with third parties.

All information entered into the app, including client names, appointments, services, notes, and backup files, is stored locally on the barber’s device.

Data Storage

Data created and used within Barber Master Suite remains on the user’s device unless the user manually exports or backs it up locally.

The app does not use cloud storage to save or sync this data.

Backups and Exports

Barber Master Suite may allow the user to create local backup or export files, such as PDF documents or device-based backup files. These files are generated and stored locally on the device under the user’s control.

If the user restores app data, restoration is performed from a backup file previously created and stored by the user on their own device.

Third-Party Sharing

Barber Master Suite does not share user data with third parties.

Permissions

If the app requests access to device storage or files, it is used only to support local features such as exporting documents, saving backups, or restoring previously created backup files.

Children’s Privacy

Barber Master Suite is not designed for children and is intended for adult users managing their business operations.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
