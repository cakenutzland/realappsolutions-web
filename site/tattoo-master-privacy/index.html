Privacy Policy

Tattoo Master — Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Tattoo Master collects the following information to provide studio management services:

  • Account Information: Email address and password for studio owners/artists.
  • Studio Information: Studio name, city, phone, working hours, and services offered.
  • Client Information: Client names, phone numbers, email addresses, and appointment history as entered by studio owners.
  • Appointment Data: Date, time, service type, assigned artist, and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Tattoo Master application
  • Enable appointment booking and studio management features
  • Process subscription payments through Google Play
  • Send appointment notifications (when enabled)
  • Improve our services

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security policies. Each studio's data is isolated and only accessible to authorized users (studio owner and their artists).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is only shared with:

  • Supabase: Our database provider (data hosting)
  • Google Play: For subscription payment processing
  • Vercel: Our web hosting provider

5. Client Booking Data

When clients book appointments through the app, their name and contact information is stored in the studio owner's account. Clients do not need to create an account to book.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), row-level security policies, and secure authentication via Supabase Auth.

7. Children's Privacy

Tattoo Master is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Studio owners can delete client data directly within the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
